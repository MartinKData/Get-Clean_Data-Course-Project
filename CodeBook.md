## Variables of the Tidy Data Sets

The variables in the tidy data set are mostly numeric representing diverse mean and standard deviation values between -1 and 1 based on diverse movement variables measured with the Samsung Galaxy S II. Subject indicates the id of the individual subjects and is thus integer. activityNames is a factor variable with six levels as shown below.
